## Introduction
---
Octopus Analog Rotation Brick is micro:bit compatible and can be rotated up to 300-degree. It is based on the potentiometer. 

 ![](https://i.imgur.com/hkPYQxY.jpg)

## Characteristics
---
- The 3-pins ports is easy to plug and play.
