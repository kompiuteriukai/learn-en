## Introduction
---
- Octopus Passive Buzzer Brick is based on a passive buzzer, it could control the volume by PWM. 

 ![](https://i.imgur.com/0G3EEXg.jpg)

## Characteristics
---
- The 3-pins ports is easy to plug and play.
- It can work with micro:bit in 3V.
